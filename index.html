Population Shifts in Malaysia: Visualizing Key Trends (1970–2024)

This visualization explores Malaysia’s population trends from 1970 to 2024, providing insights into growth, diversity, and demographic shifts across states. Through four key graphs, we’ll examine state populations, ethnic distributions, and age structures, offering a complete picture of the country's evolving population landscape.

State Population Distribution in Malaysia (2024)
The map displays Malaysia’s state populations for 2024, highlighting differences in population size. It shows how urban hubs like Selangor and Kuala Lumpur dominate in numbers, offering a snapshot of Malaysia’s demographic spread and urbanization trends.
Population Growth Trends Across Malaysian States (1970-2024)
This line graph tracks population growth across states from 1970 to 2024. It reveals long-term trends, showing rapid growth in urban areas while rural regions remain more static. This provides historical context for understanding regional demographic shifts.
Ethnic Diversity Across Malaysian States (Annual Distribution)
The pie chart illustrates the ethnic composition of each state, showcasing Malaysia’s multicultural fabric. By visualizing changes over time, it highlights shifts in ethnic proportions driven by migration and societal changes in different regions.
Age and Sex Demographics of Malaysian States (Annual Overview)
The population pyramid visualizes the age and sex distribution per state, showing how the demographic structure evolves. It highlights aging trends in some states and youthful populations in others, offering insights into workforce and social dynamics.
